# Training: The Overall Investment Slide

The **Overall Investment** slide is the money story's closer — the **Design &
Feasibility Retainer**, the projected construction range, and the **Total
Project Investment**. It pairs with **Investment by Space** (the per-space
breakdown); this slide lands the number and frames the retainer as the next
step.

---

## 1. Where the data comes from

The **retainer amount** (project retainer settings), the **construction
range** (sum of room totals), and the **hourly design rate** (Company
Settings) sync automatically. You can override the construction Low/High and
the rounding in the inspector — dollar figures are **never** invented by AI.

---

## 2. Choose a layout

**In the app:** Inspector → **Layout**. Three options:

| Layout | Looks like | Best for |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| **Three-Band Summary** (default) | Retainer band (amount + benefits ✓ list) → Projected Construction band → Total Project Investment band | The classic numbers recap |
| **Insurance Policy** | Big statement ("The $X design investment … unlocks our Zero Change Order Guarantee"), shield + umbrella over a blueprint, orange-dot benefits | Selling **why** the retainer exists |
| **Retainer CTA** | White card with orange top stripe: deliverables grid, "Design Retainer: $X (credited toward your total)", "Sign the Initial Contract today…" | The close — ask for the signature |

> **Tip:** the headline is shared across layouts — retitle it when you switch
> (e.g. "The Design Retainer as an Insurance Policy" or "The Next Step:
> Bringing [project] to Life").

---

## 3. The shared Benefits list

One **Bullets** editor feeds all three layouts: the Band-1 ✓ list, the
Insurance Policy's orange-dot bullets, and the CTA card's deliverables grid.
Edit, reorder, add, or remove in the inspector; per-bullet styling has a
"lock styles together" toggle.

---

## 4. Layout-specific controls

**In the app (Inspector — sections appear per layout):**

- **Three-Band:** Section Label, Tagline, Retainer Label/Description,
  Construction Label, Total Label/Amount — full font/size/color per band, plus
  Amount + **Round to** controls.
- **Insurance Policy:** Statement override (blank = default with the amount and
  "Zero Change Order Guarantee" highlighted in accent) + Statement / Bullet /
  Graphic size sliders.
- **Retainer CTA:** Subtitle, Retainer note (blank composes "(Credited as part
  of your total project investment of $X – $Y)"), Call-to-action line,
  Thank-you line + Card text slider. Set a **Background** photo for the
  wood-grain look — the white card floats over it.

---

## 5. AI Edit

**In the app:** the **✦ AI Edit** box at the top of the inspector. Plain
English — *"switch to the insurance policy layout and retitle it
accordingly,"* *"tighten the benefit bullets,"* or *"add a warm background."*
**↶ Undo** reverts. Dollar figures are off-limits to the AI by design.

---

## 6. Quick-start checklist

1. Confirm the retainer settings + room totals (the amounts sync from them).
2. Pick a **Layout** and **retitle the headline** to match.
3. Tidy the **Bullets** (they feed every layout).
4. Layout extras: statement wording (Insurance) or the CTA lines + a
   background photo (Retainer CTA).
5. Check the rounding ("Round to") so the figures read clean.
