# Training: The Before / After Slide

The **Before / After** slide is HHI's signature page — the client's *actual* room
photo beside (or under) an **AI-generated "after"** of the finished space. No
competitor's auto-generated deck can do this. Lead with it.

---

## 1. Where it lives

**In the app:** open a project → **Deck** tab → the **Before / After** slides sit
right after the Scope section. One is created **automatically for every room that
has an AI render + a "before" photo** — so the more rooms you render in the
Studio, the more transformations appear.

> **Heads-up:** No Before/After slide for a room? It needs **both** a *before
> photo* and a *finished render*. Add the photo on **Build Presentation / Media**
> and generate the render on **Build Presentation → Before/After renders**.

---

## 2. Pick the room + photos

**In the app (Inspector):**

- **Room** — choose which room this slide shows. The before photo and the room's
  selected render fill in automatically.
- **Before / After photos** — override either image from the room's library if
  the auto-pick isn't the best shot.

---

## 3. Choose a layout

**In the app:** Inspector → **Layout**. Six options:

| Layout | Looks like | Best for |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| **Reveal Slider** | One image with a **draggable handle** that wipes the After over the Before | Presenting live on screen — the "wow" |
| **Side by Side** | Before and After in equal columns | Clear, classic comparison |
| **After Emphasis** | Big hero After + a small Before inset on a dark panel | Showing off the render |
| **Cards** | Two rounded cards with BEFORE / AFTER pills | Clean and friendly |
| **Offset** | Before card behind, After card overlapping in front | A sense of "progression" |
| **Diagonal** | Angled BEFORE / AFTER panels with ribbon labels | Bold, high-energy |

> **Tip — Reveal Slider:** drag the white handle on the slide to wipe between the
> two images. Set where it starts with **Transformation → Reveal start** in the
> inspector. As you slide fully to one edge, the label for the hidden side fades
> away. (In a PDF/print export it freezes at the start position.)

---

## 4. The transformation stat

**In the app:** Inspector → **Transformation → Stat chip**.

A short, punchy metric shown as an accent pill, e.g. *"+168 SF"* or
*"ceilings 8′ → 18′"*. Optional but it makes the change land instantly.

---

## 5. Caption & change-list

Beneath the photos every layout shows a compact **change-list** so the imagery
stays large:

- **Bullets** (preferred) — short client-facing changes, auto-generated from the
  room's **Render Controls** checklist; your manual edits stick across re-syncs.
- **Caption** — if a room has no bullets, a one-line caption shows instead
  (auto-filled from the room's scope).
- **Sizing** — **Caption → Size** scales the change-list text (bullets *and*
  caption) on every layout.

---

## 6. AI Edit

**In the app:** the **✦ AI Edit** box at the top of the inspector. Type a change
in plain English — e.g. *"rename this to Primary Suite, add a '+220 SF' stat, and
switch to the reveal slider."* It can edit the room title, caption, before/after
labels, the stat, switch layout, and recolor the accent. The **↶ Undo** button
reverts the last change.

> **Heads-up:** AI Edit doesn't swap the *photos* (those come from the Room +
> render pickers above) — it handles the words, stat, layout, and color.

---

## 7. Quick-start checklist

1. Make sure the room has a **before photo** + a **render** (Build Presentation).
2. Open the **Before / After** slide for that room.
3. Pick a **Layout** (try **Reveal Slider** for live presenting).
4. Add a **Transformation stat** and tidy the **caption**.
5. Confirm the before/after photos are the best shots.
