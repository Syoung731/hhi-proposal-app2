# Training: The Why Us Slide

The **Why Us** slide makes the case for HHI — why a client should choose a
design‑build partner over a traditional builder. It has two families of layouts:
**grids** (your Value Pillars as a 2×2) and **comparisons** (Traditional vs. HHI),
plus a **testimonials** option. There's one Why Us slide per deck, included by
default, and it ships with the **Guarantee Grid** layout.

---

## 1. Where it lives

**In the app:** open a project → **Deck** tab → the **Why Us** slide.

---

## 2. Choose a layout

**In the app:** Inspector → **Layout**. Six options:

| Layout | Looks like | Best for |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| **Guarantee Grid** | Dark panel, 2×2 of pillars with isometric icons + an orange cross divider | The signature "HHI Builders Guarantee" — great default |
| **Advantage Grid** | A 2×2 of orange‑headed advantages over a darkened **photo** | A bold, photo‑backed statement |
| **Comparison Table** | A bordered matrix: row label · Traditional (gray) · HHI (white, big orange ✓) | A point‑by‑point, scannable contrast |
| **Comparison Columns** | Full‑bleed split — gray "Traditional" half vs. white "HHI" half, framed cards | A clean "Chaos vs. Certainty" story |
| **Comparison Cards** | Two columns of paired cards (gray vs. orange‑edged white) with ✓ | A softer card version of the comparison |
| **Testimonials Split** | Client quote cards beside a dark pillar‑highlight panel | When you have strong client quotes |

> **Tip:** the grids use your **Value Pillars**; the comparison layouts use
> their own **Traditional‑vs‑HHI rows**. Switching between the two families keeps
> both sets of content — nothing is lost.

---

## 3. The grids (Guarantee / Advantage)

These pull from your **Value Pillars** (Settings → Value Pillars). The 2×2 shows
up to four.

**In the app (Inspector):**
- **Section Title** — the big heading (e.g. "The HHI Builders Guarantee"), with
  full font / size / color / style controls in the **Content** section.
- **Pillars** — pick which pillars show (the picker), and edit each pillar's
  **title** and **description** below.
- **Sizes** — **Icon size**, **Title size**, **Description size**, and **Cross
  thickness** sliders (the orange cross divider).

**Guarantee Grid icons** — each pillar gets a built‑in **isometric icon** (orange
on the dark panel), matched to the pillar by its title:
- **Use default icons** (button under Pillars) — assigns the built‑in committed
  icons in one click. Standard pillars also show them **automatically**, no click
  needed.
- **✦ Generate with AI instead** — only for custom pillars that don't match a
  built‑in icon.

> **Tip:** the Guarantee Grid title shows the **first word in orange** — phrase
> the pillar so the key word comes first ("**Zero** Change Order Guarantee").

**Advantage Grid photo** — this layout sits over the slide's background. **In the
app:** Inspector → **Background → Change** to choose the photo; a dark scrim keeps
the text legible.

---

## 4. The comparison layouts (Traditional vs. HHI)

**In the app (Inspector → Comparison Columns):**
- **Left header / Right header** — e.g. "Traditional Builders" vs. "The HHI Standard."
- **Rows** — each row has a **Row label** (matrix only), the **Traditional** pain
  point, the **HHI lead‑in** (bold), and the **HHI detail**. Add, remove, reorder.
- **Bottom line** — an optional closing promise under the comparison (clear it to
  hide).
- **Show row labels** — toggles the matrix's left label column.
- **Text Sizes** — **Headers**, **Row titles**, **Descriptions** sliders.
- **Reset** — restores the default HHI rows + headers.

---

## 5. Theme (matches the rest of the deck)

The Why Us layouts follow the deck **Theme** dropdown (Blueprint vs. Editorial)
for the comparison layouts and headers. The grids use their own dark / photo
treatments by design. Set the theme once for the whole deck.

---

## 6. AI Edit

**In the app:** the **✦ AI Edit** box at the top of the inspector. Plain English —
e.g. *"add a subtle blueprint background,"* *"make the checks teal,"* *"switch to
the comparison table,"* or *"rewrite the rows to focus on budget certainty."*
**↶ Undo** reverts the last change, and your wording is preserved unless you
explicitly ask to change it.

---

## 7. Quick‑start checklist

1. Open the **Why Us** slide.
2. Pick a **Layout** (try **Guarantee Grid**).
3. Grids: confirm your **Pillars**, click **Use default icons**, nudge the
   **Sizes** sliders. Advantage Grid: set a **Background** photo.
4. Comparisons: set the **headers**, tidy the **rows**, and the **bottom line**.
5. Set the **Section Title** styling in **Content**.
6. Confirm the deck **Theme** reads the way you want.
