# Training: The Craftsmanship Slide

The **Craftsmanship** ("Material & Assembly Standards") slide proves build
quality instead of claiming it — macro photos of dovetail joints, stone
edges, and brass hardware, with the standards we guarantee on every build.
It's the luxury differentiator: Why Us makes the promises, this slide shows
the evidence.

**In the app:** deck toolbar → **+ Add** → Optional → **+ Craftsmanship**.
It ships with six HHI standards pre-written (dovetail joinery, full-slab
stone, waterproofing membranes, finish carpentry, level-5 finishes, layered
LED detailing) — present-ready before you touch anything.

---

## 1. Layouts

| Layout | Looks like | Best for |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| **Standards Grid** (default) | Two titled standards columns ("Structural & Preparation" / "Finish & Function") + up to 6 macro detail photos | The "Built to Last" treatment — works instantly with the default copy |
| **Annotated Photo** | One hero photo with numbered callout cards and leader lines pointing at the exact details | The engineering-callout look — your best finished-detail photo as proof |

---

## 2. Standards Grid setup

- **✦ Build collage from project materials (AI)** — the one-click path:
  reviews this project's recommended materials, renders one macro close-up
  per material (~30-45s), fills the collage, and rewrites both standards
  columns to match. **Reset** restores the six HHI defaults.
- **Column titles** are editable; assign each standard to a column or leave
  on Auto (splits evenly).
- **Macro Photo Collage:** or add up to 6 of your own close-up shots
  (dovetails, edges, hardware, membrane). Tight crops of *details* read
  far more premium than wide room shots.

> **Heads-up:** the collage is what makes this layout — without photos the
> columns narrow up but the slide is intentionally photo-led. Build or add
> photos before presenting it.

## 3. Annotated Photo setup

1. **✦ Build photo from project materials (AI)** — the one-click path. It
   reviews the materials recommended in this project's estimates (the
   "- Material" line items, priciest first) plus the style preset, picks
   the 4-6 most visually distinctive, renders one hero visual showing
   them, and rewrites the callout cards to match. Takes ~20-30 seconds.
   Pick the **Build Style** first:
   - **Photo vignette** — photorealistic magazine close-up (default).
   - **Technical drawing** — exploded isometric in navy + orange line art,
     the "Anatomy of a Remodel" look.
   - **Collage** — staggered photo panels over ghosted blueprint linework.

   None of them bake text into the image — your callout cards do the
   labeling, so the copy stays editable. Prefer a real photo? **Select
   finished-detail photo** still works.
2. For each standard, check **Pin on photo** and slide the X/Y until the
   numbered pin touches the detail (the countertop edge, the cabinet
   hardware, the tile).
3. Leader lines connect each card to its pin automatically; use **Card
   Side** to balance the columns.

> **Heads-up:** the AI build replaces the standards list with
> material-specific callouts. **Reset** restores the six HHI defaults.

---

## 4. Controls + AI Edit

Headline / section label / intro, per-standard title + description, Text
Size and Pin Size sliders, and **Reset** to restore the six HHI defaults.
**AI Edit** can rewrite the standards copy ("make these more concrete," "add
a tile-leveling standard") — photos and pins are never touched.

> **Tip:** build a small library of macro craftsmanship shots from finished
> jobs (10 minutes with a phone on your best details). One good set serves
> every proposal.
